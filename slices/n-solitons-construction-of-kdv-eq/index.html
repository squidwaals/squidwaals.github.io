构造KdV方程的N孤子解

Apr 05, 2025

最近看了一点李翊神的《孤子与可积系统》，发现用达布变换构造的KdV方程的N孤子解形式很规则，感觉很新奇。但书中没有给出证明，这里试着自己推一遍。

前置信息

假定$\phi$满足谱方程$L\phi=\lambda \phi$，同时又满足$\phi_t=A\phi$，可以导出$L,A$满足拉克斯方程$L_t =[A,L]$。

若选取$L$为薛定谔算子$L=\partial_{xx}+u(x,t)$，$A$为反对称算子$A=-4\left(\partial_{xxx}+\frac{3}{4}u\partial_x+\partial_x \frac{3}{4}u\cdot\right)$，代入拉克斯方程可以得到$u$满足KdV方程

$$ u_t + u_{xxx} + 6 u u_x = 0 $$

$L\phi=\lambda\phi$对$x$求导后，代入$\phi_t=A\phi$中消去$\phi_{xxx}$项，得到$\phi$满足的另一个方程

$$ \phi_t = u_x\phi - (4\lambda+2u)\phi_x $$

这个推导可以逆过来，从$\phi$满足$L\phi=\lambda\phi$和$\phi_t = u_x\phi - (4\lambda+2u)\phi_x$可以得出$\phi_t=A\phi$，从而导出$u$满足KdV方程。

达布定理

假定${\phi,u}$是满足如下方程的一组解

$$ \begin{cases} \phi_{xx}+u\phi=\lambda \phi \\ \phi_t = u_x\phi - (4\lambda+2u)\phi_x \\ \Rightarrow (u_t + u_{xxx} + 6 u u_x = 0) \end{cases} $$

${f,u}$是谱参数不同的另一组解

$$ \begin{cases} f_{xx}+uf=\lambda_1 f \\ f_t = u_x f - (4\lambda_1+2u)f_x \\ \Rightarrow (u_t + u_{xxx} + 6 u u_x = 0) \end{cases} $$

作达布变换

$$ \begin{cases} \bar{\phi} = \phi_x - \frac{f_x}{f}\phi \\ \bar{u} = u + 2[\ln f]_{xx} \end{cases} $$

可以证明${\bar{\phi},\bar{u}}$也满足谱参数为$\lambda$的方程

$$ \begin{cases} \bar{\phi}_{xx}+\bar{u}\bar{\phi}=\lambda \bar{\phi} \\ \bar{\phi}_t = \bar{u}_x\bar{\phi} - (4\lambda+2\bar{u})\bar{\phi}_x \\ \Rightarrow (\bar{u}_t + \bar{u}_{xxx} + 6 \bar{u} \bar{u}_x = 0) \end{cases} $$

于是通过这种方式就实现了从KdV方程的一个解$u$出发构造另一个解$\bar{u}$。

双孤子解

选取初始种子$u[0]=0$，对应的解$\phi[0]=f(\lambda)$。记$f(\lambda_1)$为$u=0$时另一个谱参数$\lambda_1$下的解，那么由达布变换

$$ \begin{cases} \phi[1]= f_x(\lambda) - \frac{f_x(\lambda_1)}{f(\lambda_1)}f(\lambda) = \frac{f_x(\lambda)f(\lambda_1)-f(\lambda)f_x(\lambda_1)}{f(\lambda_1)} \\ u[1]= 2[\ln f(\lambda_1)]_{xx} \end{cases} $$

${\phi[1],u[1]}$满足

$$ \begin{cases} \phi[1]_{xx}+u[1]\phi[1]=\lambda \phi[1] \\ \phi[1]_t = u[1]_x\phi[1] - (4\lambda+2u[1])\phi[1]_x \\ \Rightarrow (u[1]_t + u[1]_{xxx} + 6 u[1] u[1]_x = 0) \end{cases} $$

由于$u[1]$和$\lambda$没有关系，容易看出可以存在$f[1]$满足

$$ \begin{cases} f[1]_{xx}+u[1]f[1]=\lambda_2 f[1] \\ f[1]_t = u[1]_x f[1] - (4\lambda_2+2u[1])f[1]_x \\ \Rightarrow (u[1]_t + u[1]_{xxx} + 6 u[1] u[1]_x = 0) \end{cases} $$$$ f[1]= \frac{f_x(\lambda_2)f(\lambda_1)-f(\lambda_2)f_x(\lambda_1)}{f(\lambda_1)} $$

其中$f(\lambda_2)$是$u=0$时谱参数为$\lambda_2$时的解。于是我们可以利用$\phi[1],u[1],f[1]$继续做达布变换得到双孤子解

$$ \begin{cases} \phi[2]= \phi_x[1] - \frac{f_x[1]}{f[1]}\phi[1]= \frac{ \left| \begin{array}{ccc} f(\lambda_1) &f_x(\lambda_1) &f_{xx}(\lambda_1) \\ f(\lambda_2) &f_x(\lambda_2) &f_{xx}(\lambda_2) \\ f(\lambda) &f_x(\lambda) &f_{xx}(\lambda) \end{array}\right| } {\left| \begin{array}{cc} f(\lambda_1) &f_x(\lambda_1) \\ f(\lambda_2) &f_x(\lambda_2) \end{array}\right|}=\frac{w[f(\lambda_1),f(\lambda_2),f(\lambda)]}{w[f(\lambda_1),f(\lambda_2)]} \\ u[2]= u[1] + 2[\ln f[1]]_{xx} = 2\left[\ln \left| \begin{array}{cc} f(\lambda_1) & f_x(\lambda_1) \\ f(\lambda_2) & f_x(\lambda_2) \end{array}\right|\right]_{xx}=2[\ln w[f(\lambda_1),f(\lambda_2)]]_{xx} \end{cases} $$

其中$w[\cdots]$是朗斯基行列式。

N孤子解

从上面的结果可以猜测$n$孤子解的形式

$$ \begin{cases} \phi[n] = \frac{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda)]}{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)]} \\ u[n] = 2[\ln w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)]]_{xx} \end{cases} $$

下面将通过归纳法证明这个式子，不过在此之前先推导一些朗斯基行列式相关的公式。

朗斯基行列式求导

$$ w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)]=\left| \begin{array}{c} f(\lambda_1) &\cdots &f^{(n-1)}(\lambda_1) &f^{(n)}(\lambda_1) \\ f(\lambda_2) &\cdots &f^{(n-1)}(\lambda_2) &f^{(n)}(\lambda_2) \\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ f(\lambda_n) &\cdots &f^{(n-1)}(\lambda_n) &f^{(n)}(\lambda_n) \end{array} \right| $$

将朗斯基行列式展开为$n!$项，对每一个乘积项应用莱布尼兹法则，再按照求导作用到的位置将所得到的$n\times n!$项分类，恢复成$n$个行列式，可以发现其中有$n-1$个行列式均为零（因为有相同的列），所以朗斯基行列式求导等于

$$ w_x[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)]=\left| \begin{array}{c} f(\lambda_1) &\cdots &f^{(n-1)}(\lambda_1) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_1) \\ f(\lambda_2) &\cdots &f^{(n-1)}(\lambda_2) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_2) \\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ f(\lambda_n) &\cdots &f^{(n-1)}(\lambda_n) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_n) \end{array} \right| $$

分块矩阵行列式

分块矩阵求行列式有如下公式

$$ \left| \begin{array}{c} A & B \\ C & D \end{array} \right| = |A| |D - C A^{-1} B| $$

应用到朗斯基行列式中有

$$ \begin{aligned} w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda_{n+1})]&=\left| \begin{array}{c} f(\lambda_1) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_1) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_1) \\ f(\lambda_2) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_2) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_2)\\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ f(\lambda_n) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_n) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_n)\\ f(\lambda_{n+1}) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_{n+1}) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_{n+1}) \end{array} \right|\\ &=w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)] ( f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_{n+1}) - C(\lambda_{n+1},n) W^{-1}(f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)) B(n+1,\lambda_{n}) ) \end{aligned} $$$$ \begin{aligned} w_x[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda_{n+1})]&=\left| \begin{array}{c} f(\lambda_1) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_1) &f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_1) \\ f(\lambda_2) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_2) &f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_2)\\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ f(\lambda_n) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_n) &f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_n)\\ f(\lambda_{n+1}) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_{n+1}) &f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_{n+1}) \end{array} \right|\\ &=w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)] ( f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_{n+1}) - C(\lambda_{n+1},n) W^{-1}(f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)) B(n+2,\lambda_{n}) ) \end{aligned} $$$$ \begin{aligned} w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda)]&=\left| \begin{array}{c} f(\lambda_1) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_1) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_1) \\ f(\lambda_2) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_2) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_2)\\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ f(\lambda_n) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_n) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_n)\\ f(\lambda) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda) \end{array} \right|\\ &=w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)] ( f^{(n+1)}(\lambda) - C(\lambda,n) W^{-1}(f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)) B(n+1,\lambda_{n}) ) \end{aligned} $$$$ \begin{aligned} w_x[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda)]&=\left| \begin{array}{c} f(\lambda_1) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_1) &f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_1) \\ f(\lambda_2) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_2) &f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_2)\\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ f(\lambda_n) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_n) &f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_n)\\ f(\lambda) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda) &f^{(n+2)}(\lambda) \end{array} \right|\\ &=w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)] ( f^{(n+2)}(\lambda) - C(\lambda,n) W^{-1}(f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)) B(n+2,\lambda_{n}) ) \end{aligned} $$$$ \begin{aligned} w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda_{n+1}), f(\lambda)]&=\left| \begin{array}{c} f(\lambda_1) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_1) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_1) &f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_1) \\ f(\lambda_2) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_2) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_2) &f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_2) \\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ f(\lambda_n) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_n) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_n) &f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_n) \\ f(\lambda_{n+1}) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda_{n+1}) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_{n+1}) &f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_{n+1}) \\ f(\lambda) &\cdots &f^{(n)}(\lambda) &f^{(n+1)}(\lambda) &f^{(n+2)}(\lambda) \end{array} \right|\\ &=w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)]\left| \left( \begin{matrix} f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_{n+1}) & f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_{n+1}) \\ f^{(n+1)}(\lambda) & f^{(n+2)}(\lambda) \end{matrix} \right)- \left( \begin{matrix} C(\lambda_{n+1},n) \\ C(\lambda,n) \end{matrix} \right) W^{-1}(f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)) \left( \begin{matrix} B(n+1,\lambda_{n}) & B(n+2,\lambda_{n}) \end{matrix} \right) \right| \\ &=w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)] \left| \begin{array}{c} f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_{n+1}) - C(\lambda_{n+1},n) W^{-1}(f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)) B(n+1,\lambda_{n}) & f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_{n+1}) - C(\lambda_{n+1},n) W^{-1}(f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)) B(n+2,\lambda_{n}) \\ f^{(n+1)}(\lambda) - C(\lambda,n) W^{-1}(f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)) B(n+1,\lambda_{n}) & f^{(n+2)}(\lambda) - C(\lambda,n) W^{-1}(f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)) B(n+2,\lambda_{n}) \end{array} \right| \\ &= \frac{w_x[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda)]w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda_{n+1})] - w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda)] w_x[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda_{n+1})]}{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)]} \end{aligned} $$

其中

$$ \begin{aligned} W(f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)) & =\left( \begin{array}{c} f(\lambda_1) &\cdots &f^{(n-1)}(\lambda_1) &f^{(n)}(\lambda_1) \\ f(\lambda_2) &\cdots &f^{(n-1)}(\lambda_2) &f^{(n)}(\lambda_2) \\ \vdots &\vdots &\vdots &\vdots \\ f(\lambda_n) &\cdots &f^{(n-1)}(\lambda_n) &f^{(n)}(\lambda_n) \end{array} \right) \\ C(\lambda_{n+1},n) &= \left( \begin{matrix} f(\lambda_{n+1}) &\cdots &f^{(n-1)}(\lambda_{n+1}) &f^{(n)}(\lambda_{n+1}) \end{matrix} \right) \\ C(\lambda,n) &= \left( \begin{matrix} f(\lambda) &\cdots &f^{(n-1)}(\lambda) &f^{(n)}(\lambda) \end{matrix} \right) \\ B(n+1,\lambda_{n}) &= \left( \begin{matrix} f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_1) \\ \vdots \\ f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_{n-1}) \\ f^{(n+1)}(\lambda_{n}) \end{matrix} \right) \\ B(n+2,\lambda_{n}) &= \left( \begin{matrix} f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_1) \\ \vdots \\ f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_{n-1}) \\ f^{(n+2)}(\lambda_{n}) \end{matrix} \right) \end{aligned} $$

归纳证明

假定$n$孤子解有如下形式

$$ \begin{cases} \phi[n] = \frac{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda)]}{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)]} \\ u[n] = 2[\ln w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)]]_{xx} \end{cases} $$

现在证明利用$f[n] = \frac{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_{n}), f(\lambda_{n+1})]}{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_{n})]}$进行达布变换后的$n+1$孤子解为

$$ \begin{cases} \phi[n+1] = \frac{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_{n+1}), f(\lambda)]}{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_{n+1})]} \\ u[n+1] = 2[\ln w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_{n+1})]]_{xx} \end{cases} $$

$u[n+1]$的证明是显然的，所以下面主要关注$\phi[n+1]$

$$ \begin{aligned} & \phi[n+1] =\phi_x[n] - \frac{f_x[n]}{f[n]}\phi[n] \\ & = \frac{w_x[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda)]w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)] - w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda)] w_x[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)]}{w^2[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)]} \\ & \quad - \frac{w_x[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda_{n+1})]w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)] - w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda_{n+1})] w_x[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)]}{w^2[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)]} \frac{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_{n}),f(\lambda)]}{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_{n}),f(\lambda_{n+1})]} \\ & = \frac{w_x[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda)]w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_{n}),f(\lambda_{n+1})]-w_x[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n), f(\lambda_{n+1})]w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_{n}),f(\lambda)]}{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_n)] w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_{n}),f(\lambda_{n+1})]} \\ \end{aligned} $$

利用上一小节证明的式子可以得到

$$ \phi[n+1]= \frac{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_{n+1}), f(\lambda)]}{w[f(\lambda_1),\cdots,f(\lambda_{n+1})]} $$

这正是我们想要的结论。

题外话-贝克隆变换和非线性叠加

另一种形式的$n$孤子解构造可以通过贝克隆变换后的非线性叠加公式得到，非常有趣。

定义$w$满足方程$w_t+3w_x^2+w_{xxx}=0$，对它求导可以得到$u=w_x$满足KdV方程。将上面的达布变换应用到$w$上

$$ \begin{cases} \bar{u} = u + 2(\ln f)_{xx} \\ (\bar{w}=w + 2(\ln f)_x) \\ f_{xx} + uf = \lambda f \end{cases} \Rightarrow \bar{w}_x-w_x=2\frac{f_{xx}f - f^2_x}{f^2}=2\lambda-2w_x-\frac{1}{2}(\bar{w}-w)^2 $$$$ \Rightarrow \bar{w}_x + w_x = 2\lambda - \frac{1}{2}(\bar{w}-w)^2 $$

达布变换后$\bar{w}$依然满足$\bar{w}_t+3\bar{w}x^2+\bar{w}{xxx}=0$，和$w$满足的方程作差得到

$$ \bar{w}_t - w_t + 3\bar{w}^2_x - 3 w^2_x + \bar{w}_{xxx} - w_{xxx} = 0 $$

这两个方程允许我们从$w$得到$\bar{w}$。

假定我们从种子$w_0$出发通过参数为$\lambda_1$（$\lambda_2$）的变换得到了解$w_1$（$w_2$），然后再利用$w_1$通过参数为$\lambda_2$（$\lambda_1$）的变换得到了解$w_{12}$（$w_{21}$），令这两个解相等，就可以得到非线性叠加公式（也即从$w_1,w_2$出发代数叠加得到$w_{12}$）

$$ w_{12} = w_0 + \frac{4(\lambda_2 - \lambda_1)}{w_2 - w_1} $$

这种叠加可以一直进行下去构造多孤子解，如图所示 可以看出，非线性叠加的参数对称化要求（$w_{21}=w_{12}$）对于这种持续的构造是必要的，因为需要不同的参数解作为种子。
